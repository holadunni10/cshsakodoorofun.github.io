About Our School

Shaping Futures in
Ibeju-Lekki

Community Senior High School, Akodo-Orofun is a government secondary school committed to providing quality education to students in Ibeju-Lekki, Lagos State. We pride ourselves on academic excellence, strong moral values, and the all-round development of our students.

  • 🎯

    Our Mission

    To provide qualitative and morally sound education that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed for lifelong success.

  • 🌟

    Our Vision

    To be a centre of excellence and model school that produces well-rounded, nationally competitive graduates from the heart of Ibeju-Lekki.

  • 📚

    Our Core Values

    Excellence · Integrity · Discipline · Innovation · Community Service

Principal's Message

Welcome to Our School Community

On behalf of our dedicated staff and vibrant student community, I warmly welcome you to Community Senior High School, Akodo-Orofun. Our school is not just an institution — it is a family committed to nurturing every child's potential. We believe that every student has unique talents, and our role is to discover and develop those gifts through academic rigour, discipline, and character building. We invite parents, guardians, and the broader community to partner with us in this noble mission of shaping the leaders of tomorrow.

— Mr. Awoniran J.A., Principal

Departments & Subjects

We offer a broad curriculum aligned with the Nigerian national standards, designed to prepare students for WAEC, NECO, and tertiary education.

🔬

Science Department

Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Agricultural Science, Further Mathematics. Led by Mrs. Ojo (HOD).

📖

Humanities Department

English Language, Literature, History, Government, CRS, Social Studies. Led by Mr. Onakoya (HOD).

💻

ICT & Digital Technology

Computer Studies, Digital Technology, and career guidance. Taught by Mr. Oladunni O.E.

💼

Business Department

Commerce, Financial Accounting, Typewriting, and Business Studies. Led by Mrs. Tajudeen.

🧮

Mathematics & Basic Science

Core Mathematics, Basic Science, and Integrated Science coordinated under the Vice Principal (Academics).

🎨

Student Counseling

Career guidance, mental wellness, and educational counseling offered by Mr. Oladunni O.E. — School Counselor.
